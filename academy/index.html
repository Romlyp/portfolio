Наша мета - сталий розвиток талантів у баскетболістів у Вінниці. Наші табори орієнтовані на вікові групи від U12 до U18. Незалежно від того, чи є ви спортсменом, що змагається, чи просто любителем баскетболу. З нами ви в руках професіоналів. Спортивна підтримка молодих талантів через індивідуальні тренування, командні тренування та баскетбольні табори

Тренера баскетбольної академії

Maria Laterza - Advisor to the founder/Head of Elite Basketball Operations & Events

Maria joins the Brooklyn Basketball Academy as a consultant after a tenure in the NBA League Office working in International Basketball Operations. In her time at the NBA she helped grow the game globally, working on special projects such as Basketball Without Borders and was part of the core team that launched the NBA Academy globally in 2016. Maria is a native of South Brooklyn, who played for Bishop Kearney High School and was all-city ranked player. She played college ball at the best mid-major in the country, Marist College. Winning 4 consecutive MAAC Tournament Championships with 4 NCAA March Madness tournament appearances, 2 first round wins, and captain of most winning team in program history. After college she went on to play professionally Europe for the Italian National team, competing on FIBA’s world stage while playing for various club teams in Italy for a decade before being recruited to work in the NBA to scout and cultivate the best future young talent the world can offer. At the Brooklyn Basketball Academy, Maria serves as advisor to our founder, and oversee's basketball operations and events using her expertise and deep industry knowledge of men’s and women’s from grassroots to pro, to help our athletes excel on and off the court.
